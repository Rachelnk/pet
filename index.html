ZAVI

About pet

Zavi is a year and a month old male cat.
He's a European shorthair cat and grey and white in color.
Like most house cats he enjoys a 12-16 hour sleep time.

For physical stimulation, AKA playtime he runs around the house, playing with yarn and toys, hiding behind crutains.
His diet consists of omena, mandazi, chapati supringsly enough. He can't consume cow's milk unfortunately.

He dislikes visits to the vet , being indoors for too long, as well as baths.

He's a fast learner, he learned how to open window latches just through observation. He can also open lower cabinets

He has scratched almost all the sofas and curtains, gone number 2 all over the house
but he was abandoned as a kitten and we gave him a home, love and sofas to destroy.

Owning a pet can sometimes be overwhelming but they need patience, caring, understanding (since they're different from us) and love.
Remember to take your furry friend to the vet regularly.
